Domestic Violence Resources

If you or someone you know is experiencing domestic violence, contact the National Domestic Violence Hotline at 1-800-799-7233 / TTY: 1-800-787-3224 or text "START" to 88788.

There are also other ways to report abuse:

  • Contact the Victim Services Unit or Special Crimes Unit of your local police department, if these are available
  • Call a domestic violence hotline to arrange a safe meeting with an officer at a shelter or support service
  • Make a report directly at the hospital or police station

Local Domestic Violence Organizations

Domestic violence organizations offer a variety of services, no matter what your situation might be. You can reach out to 24/7 hotlines for immediate assistance, seek help with legal matters, or find safe and secure housing. These organizations are here to guide, support, and protect you every step of the way. The following are a small collection of domestic violence organizations that exist in Southern California.
